Summary

The Shawshank Redemption is a 1994 drama film about a banker named Andy Dufresne, who is wrongfully convicted of murder and sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary. Over the course of his time in prison, he forms a close friendship with fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding. Andy uses his intelligence to help others and eventually plots his escape, making the film a powerful story of hope, friendship, and survival.
